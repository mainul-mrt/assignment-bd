Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovations

Innovation is defined as the process of bringing about new ideas, methods, products, services, or solutions that have a significant positive impact and value.

Sustainability

Sustainability is a way of using resources without depleting them or causing harm to the environment or people.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the set of facilities and systems that serve a country, city, or other area, and encompasses the services and facilities necessary for its economy, households and firms to function.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of gaining the knowledge, skills, and resources to make decisions and take action, especially in controlling one's life and claiming one's rights.
